Who We Are

There has been a lot of speculation and debate about who the best ping pong player is at Code Fellows. So we created a web page that keeps track of wins and losses and ranks hackers accordingly. So create a profile and pick up a paddle, let's rally!

House Rules

- To add an official win to your record you must win a best of three series.

- Games are played to 11 and you must win by 2 points.

- Alternate the server every 2 points.

- You cannot lose a game on a serve.
